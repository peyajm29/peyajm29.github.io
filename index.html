Peya Mowar

I am a research fellow at Microsoft Research India (MSRI). I work in Technology and Empowerment with Dr. Saikat Guha and Tanuja Ganu.

At MSRI, I build multi-lingual document intelligence systems for making long-form documents, such as newspapers, accessible to people with print impairment. More recently, I am studying the coping mechanisms employed by screenreader users while navigating news from online sources with Dr. Mohit Jain.

Previously, I have been fortunate to work with Prof. Khurshid Ahmad at Trinity College Dublin. I graduated with distinct honors from Delhi Technological University, India where my bachelor's thesis was advised by Prof. D.K. Vishwakarma.

My research interest lies at the intersection of Responsible Artificial Intelligence and Human-Computer Interaction.

I am applying to PhD programs for Fall'23!
